How We Work

The Design & Execution Journey

From the initial consultation to handing over the keys, we keep the process transparent and structured.

01

Initial Consultation

We discuss your spatial needs, design preferences, budget expectations, and initial Vastu principles.

02

2D Layout & Mood Board

Our designers outline optimized 2D layout drafts and gather material palettes to set the tone.

03

3D Visuals & Selection

We generate photorealistic 3D renders so you see your space. Next, we finalize and lock materials.

04

Turnkey Execution

Our skilled carpentry, civil, plumbing, painting, and electrical teams execute the plans under strict supervision.

05

Handover & Styling

We perform rigorous quality checks, run final decor styling, clean the space, and hand you the keys.
